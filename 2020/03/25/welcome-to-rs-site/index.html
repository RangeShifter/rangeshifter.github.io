Welcome to the RangeShifter website

Find the latest release and tutorials

Posted by Damaris Zurell on Wednesday, March 25, 2020

Welcome to the official website of the eco-evolutionary modelling platform “RangeShifter” (Bocedi et al. 2014). We will use this site to introduce you to the different RangeShifter features and applications, provide tutorials and further details. Also, we provide links to the latest software releases of RangeShifter and the accompanying R package RangeshiftR.

References

Bocedi, G., S.C.F. Palmer, G. Pe’er, R.K. Heikkinen, Y.G. Matsinos, K. Watts, and J.M.J. Travis. 2014. “RangeShifter: A Platform for Modelling Spatial Eco-Evolutionary Dynamics and Species’ Responses to Environmental Changes.” Methods in Ecology and Evolution 5 (4): 388–96. https://doi.org/10.1111/2041-210X.12162.
